Built for Your Happiest Days

When there was no mandapam in our community, we took a bold decision to build one that everyone could afford and be proud of. Since 2010, Manikandan Mahal has been the trusted backdrop for thousands of families' most precious celebrations.

We are a compact, beautifully maintained mandapam that doesn't compromise on comfort or warmth. Our purpose is simple: to serve our community during their happiest times, with genuine care and honest pricing.

From intimate religious gatherings to large wedding receptions, we have been honoured to be part of it all — and we continue to grow with the love and trust of every family that walks through our doors.

Venue Policies & Norms

✦ ── ✦ ── ✦

We kindly ask all guests to respect our venue rules so everyone has a safe and joyful experience.

No Alcohol or SmokingAlcohol and cigarettes are strictly not permitted inside the premises.
DJ with PermissionLoud DJ music is allowed with prior permission and compliance with local noise norms.
Outside Food & Catering WelcomeYou may bring outside food and your own catering team.
Vegetarian Cooking OnlyWe provide Cooking utilities for vegetarian food only.
